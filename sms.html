About us

Mensa Futura Life Sciences has been associated with Pharmaceutical Industry for the last 25 years.We are quality oriented pharmaceutical company in India and Have a Product portfolio that covers all major therapy area, including Anti–infective, Nutritional Supplements, Gastrointestinal, Pain management, Cardiovascular & Diabetes, Dermatology, Urology and Central Nervous System (CNS). Using world class manufacturing facilities at Gujarat and Himachal Pradesh, Mensa Futura Life Sciences is all set to meet the upcoming challenges in the field of healthcare. A highly qualified and devoted workforce has always been an asset to the company. All the departments are interlinked to each other by a latest management co-ordination system to synchronize the administrative, manufacturing, marketing and exporting process of the company. With the support of its distributors and doctors, Mensa Futura Life Sciences is all set to achieve the goals.
